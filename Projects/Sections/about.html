About Twinkling Marketing

Founded in 2019, Twinkling Marketing has been at the forefront of digital innovation, helping businesses transform their online presence and achieve unprecedented growth.

Our Story

Twinkling was born from a simple observation,too many businesses were struggling to navigate the complex world of digital marketing. Traditional marketing agencies were stuck in old ways, while businesses needed fresh, data-driven approaches to compete online.

Our founder, Sruji Sriram, saw an opportunity to bridge this gap. With her background in digital strategy and a passion for helping businesses grow, she assembled a team of experts who shared her vision of making digital marketing accessible, effective, and results-focused.

Today, we've helped over 500 businesses across various industries achieve their growth goals through innovative digital marketing strategies. From local startups to established enterprises, our approach remains the same: understand your business, craft a tailored strategy, and deliver measurable results.
